All About Who We Are and What We Do at Midlands

Founded in 2017, Midlands Language Solutions offers flexible, innovative Translation and Localization solutions that help companies and individuals drive international revenue through improved global communications. We are led by a team of seasoned & experienced localization professionals passionate about blending language with the world’s most advanced translation technology. Prior to launching Midlands Language Solutions, our team successfully delivered tens of thousands of multilingual projects for the world’s most iconic companies including TripAdvisor, Huawei, Samsung, Canva, Uber and hundreds of others. Since 2017, we’ve applied that expertise to service leading global brands including Shelf Drilling, Qatar Airways, Expedia, Amazon, Apple, Meta and King Saud University.
